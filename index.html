About

Who we are

Welcome to the International
Adventist Fellowship Gdańsk

We are a multicultural Christian family within the Seventh-day Adventist Church in Gdańsk, Poland. As different as we are, we gather together united in our savior Jesus Christ to praise God through:

  • Bible study
  • Prayer
  • Singing
  • Listening to His word
  • Christian Fellowship
  • Ministering

We encourage, lift up, and support one another while waiting for Christ imminent return.

Visiting our website or visiting our church we want you to feel part of the family of God.

We warmly invite You to worship with us.
